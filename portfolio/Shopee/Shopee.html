Shopee Product & Design Challenge

I took part in the Shopee Product & Design Challenge with a friend where we were tasked to create a new feature that would Shopee to remain the leading e-commerce platform in Singapore. I was mainly working on the problem breakdown and business case and helped out with the prototyping. We decided to create "Shopee Health" to leverage on the emerging aging population and health awareness trend in Singapore and tie it back to Shopee's main e-commerce business.
