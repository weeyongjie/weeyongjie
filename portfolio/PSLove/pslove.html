pslove - market validation project

pslove is a female period care startup with both online and off-the-shelf products. Its products range from mentrual heat-pads to the period-tracking mobile app.

As part of the NUS Overseas College TR4049 module, my team went to shopping malls and schools to interview more than 100 people to validate a set of hypotheses. We also did an analysis of their current product offerings including competitor research and ended off with new features suggestions and areas for improvements.
