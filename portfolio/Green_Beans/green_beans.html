Green Beans - Digital Fight against Climate Change

I took part in the Huawei Ideathon 2.0 the week I touched down in Beijing and was given the problem statement: "How can we mitigate Climate Change with Digital Solutions?". After a brief discussion, we decided to target the consumer end as we felt it was more feasible given our limited knowledge of the corporate green initiatives and limited timeframe we have to come up with a proposal. Our big idea is to use the concept of gamification to incentivise consumers to be more mindful of their carbon footprint and commit to daily green habits.
